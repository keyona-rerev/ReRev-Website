Building Persuasive One-Pagers with AI

The Philosophy

Your success completely lies in the agency of others. That's not me being dark, it's just reality. You have an idea, but what you don't have is allocated resources and buy-in.

Key Insight: When you show someone a visual, a clean professional one-pager or interactive pitch, it becomes real. That idea goes from intangible to concrete.

The Skill: Using AI to Generate HTML and CSS

Traditionally, creating polished custom sales materials meant either hiring a designer or spending hours in design tools like Canva. But now, with AI and some basic HTML and CSS knowledge, you can create professional branded one-pagers in minutes.

Step 1: Establish Your Copy

Start with the most important part: the content. The entire purpose of using HTML and CSS is not just to make it cute, but to make it real.

  • What problem does this solve?
  • Who's the ideal person to hear this?
  • What action do I want them to take?
  • What do they care about?
  • What's the end benefit?
  • What resources are needed to pull this off well?

Pro-tip: Use SpeechNotes (or your preferred voice-to-text tool) to freely converse your thoughts instead of typing. Not only is this faster, it's more effective.

Step 2: Generate the HTML and CSS

Once your copy is locked in, ask the AI to turn it into a finished product using HTML and CSS. You're creating a stylized, cohesive, put-together piece.

Pro tip: LLMs are really good at mimicking Apple's branding. If you want a more refined look, that's your go-to for it to emulate.

Step 3: Deploy and Customize

I usually paste the code into CodePen (free, browser-based, no setup required). You now have a live shareable link you can send to anyone.

  1. Open the "Notebook" app on your laptop
  2. Paste the code into a new file
  3. Save as *name of your choice* + ".html"
  4. Upload the HTML file to the converter

Real-World Win: We spent time on content, used AI for formatting, and won against competitors using AI presentation makers. Theirs looked cute; ours was functional and persuasive.

Your One-Pager Action Plan:

If you're currently employed: Is there an idea you're trying to get more people behind? With less than 30 minutes, you can create this one-pager and get momentum.

If you're pitching yourself: Instead of sending an email with your hand out, send something visual and interactive. Momentum is everything in business.
